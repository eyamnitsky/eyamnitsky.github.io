The ProductYou have to learn the rules of the game. And then you have to play better than anyone else. --Albert Einstein

What do most sales teams spend their time on? Sales? Nope. They are buried under neverending avalanche of administrative work. They are attending pointless meetings, sending redundant emails, and submitting annoying status reports. When do they sell, ask you? In between, and after hours. Contrary to the image of easy going, golf playing, and hand over fist money making the sales guys are busier than bees.

Our product is aiming to alliviate the pains. First, by fixing the contacts. Second, by revolutionizing the meetings. And finally, by taking over all administrative tasks. Our game-like interface connects teams and brings the selling back to their lives.
