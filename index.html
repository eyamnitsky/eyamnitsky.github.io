The Problem

                                          

What do most sales teams spend their time on? Sales? Nope. They are buried under neverending avalanche of administrative work. They are attending pointless meetings, sending redundant emails, and submitting annoying status reports. When do they sell, ask you? In between, and after hours. Contrary to the image of easy going, golf playing, and hand over fist money making the sales guys are busier than bees. And what about all the tools Sales people have in their disposal? Not so helpful...

Our product is aiming to alliviate these pains. First, by fixing the contacts. Second, by revolutionizing the meetings. And finally, by providing the critical insights into the customer. And did we mention it will take over all the administrative tasks? Our game-like interface connects Sales teams, and brings the selling back to their lives.
